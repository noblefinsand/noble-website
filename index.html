About Me

Noble Finsand

Currently I am attending school at SLCC to get my associates degree. From here, I am transferring down to Utah Valley University to get my bachelors in software engineering. Along with my schooling, I am doing an online program at Freecodecamp to expand my knowledge of computer programming. My goal is to get hired as a web developer/front end developer before I am graduated.

Growing up I loved creating new things with my computer. I first started out graphic designing, creating images with photoshop, and from there branched out. A little bit about my background, during highschool I took advanced HTML and CSS where we did the basics of web development. I also attended Canyons Technical center where I learned about networking, and how to build computers. Immediately I fell in love with web development and knew I wanted to do this for a career.

Outside of school, I manage a coffee shop called Beans and Brews where I have been a barista for almost 4 years. I definitely am a coffee snob, and I love to do amateur latte art. See my pictures below.
